About Muhammad Khawar CEO MKB Production

MKB Production is Gilgit-Baltistan's premium video production house, specializing in cinematic, high-impact visual content for government, corporate, and tourism brands.

Our experienced team delivers industry-standard services—combining compelling storytelling with world-class cinematography, sound design, and post-production. Our mission is to showcase the beauty, culture, and progress of Gilgit-Baltistan through authentic storytelling.
