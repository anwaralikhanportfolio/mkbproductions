Founder & CEO

About Muhammad Khawar Bagoro, CEO MKB Production

MKB Production is a premium video production house founded and led by Muhammad Khawar Bagoro, one of Gilgit-Baltistan's leading content creators and filmmakers. We specialize in producing cinematic, high-impact visual content for government institutions, corporate organizations, tourism brands, NGOs, and development partners.

Driven by creativity and technical excellence, our experienced team delivers industry-standard production services, combining compelling storytelling with world-class cinematography, professional sound design, and cinematic post-production.

Over the years, MKB Production has collaborated with leading organizations across Gilgit-Baltistan, producing content for government departments, the Tourism Department, Gilgit-Baltistan Police, and the GB Polo Federation. Our mission is to showcase the beauty, culture, people, and progress of Gilgit-Baltistan while helping organizations communicate their vision through authentic, cinematic storytelling.
